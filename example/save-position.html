Just orientate your smartphone and enjoy!

You should read this page on a smartphone, or any other device that has a gyroscope and supports JavaScript Orientation API.

Meanwhile your read these lines, Sphoords saves your device's orientation on a file. Go open the display-position.html file on another browser (for example on your computer): you will see a sphere where are places some points. These points follow your device's orientation. Move it to see! You can even take your mouse and move the sphere to better see your movements.
